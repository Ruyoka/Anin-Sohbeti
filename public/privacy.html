Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Anın Sohbeti, an anonymous chat service operated under aninfotografi.online. We value your privacy and are committed to maintaining transparency and user safety.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app only uses temporary session identifiers (nicknames and socket IDs) to anonymously connect users for real-time chat. These identifiers are deleted immediately after the chat session ends.

3. How We Use Information

Session identifiers are used solely to match users temporarily and enable communication during an active session. No chat logs or messages are stored on our servers.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our app and website do not use cookies, analytics, or any third-party tracking technologies.

5. User Responsibility

Users are fully responsible for the content they share during chat sessions. Inappropriate or harmful messages may lead to automatic disconnection or blocking. Please interact respectfully.

6. Children’s Privacy

This service is intended strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly allow minors to use the platform.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated without prior notice. Any updates will be posted on this page. Please review it periodically for the latest information.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@aninfotografi.online.
