Privacy Policy

Introduction

“Anın Sohbeti” is an anonymous chat service operated under aninfotografi.online. We value your privacy and are committed to maintaining transparency about how the service works.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app only uses temporary session identifiers (nicknames and socket IDs) to connect users anonymously.

How We Use Information

Temporary session data is used solely to connect users for the duration of a chat and is deleted immediately after the conversation ends.

Cookies and Tracking

The app and website do not use cookies, analytics, or third-party trackers.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the content they share. Inappropriate or offensive messages may result in disconnection or blocking from the service.

Children’s Privacy

This service is strictly intended for users aged 18 and above.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated at any time without prior notice. Please review it periodically for changes.

Contact
